Quick guide for booking on this static version

How booking works

  1. Open the Cars page and choose the model you want.
  2. Note the daily rate and your preferred pickup and return dates.
  3. Share the details through call or email from the Contact page.

This project runs as a fully static site, so there is no live form submission.
